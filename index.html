The philosophy

This is not a
water bottle.

It's a movement against the throwaway culture that is quietly drowning our cities.

Inspired by Germany's Pfand deposit system — one of the most successful circular economy programs in history — Pisto adapts it for India's streets, campuses, and communities.

We don't ask you to care about the planet. We make caring effortless, profitable, and satisfying. Behavior change through design.

"The most powerful systems are the ones that reward the behavior they want to see."
